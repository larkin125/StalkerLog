20240719 - Rostok Route

Summary

Proceeded to Rostok with Walter Shenoski

Expedition Log



1 - Farm

Walter Shenoski and I were going to head to Rostok (LITZ), he had some stuff to sell and I needed to learn the route. We were joined by another random Life In the Zone/Factions/Loner Factions/Loner we found at Rookie Village (GOC). He was a little strange and monotone and at the time we didn't realise what would happen next. Turns out the guy was Monolith (LITZ), they were after me. Turns out I am Most Wanted.

We made our way to North Farm to stop off during the dark. While there, the random Loner turned on us. Attacked and tried to rob us. I surrendered but Walter Shenoski ended up killing the guy, but his gear got wrecked. We laughed it off and headed further North.

2 - Ruin Building

WARNING Radiation / Pseudodog
We headed north through the Military checkpoint and on the road north, we were attacked by a Pseuddog. We had to run due to being close to the Bandits (LITZ).

3 - Dog fight

We went east and circled around to avoid going North past the Bandits (LITZ) HQ in the Train Yard. I went loud by accident after we were attacked by BlindDogs. We had to run as fast as we could.

4 - Flea Market

We stopped off at Flea Market (LITZ), there were two Stash's, one in the basement and one in the roof.

5 - Approach

We headed North, attacked by a few dogs. It was close to an Emission and we made a run for it. Hiding in the bunker just outside the entrance.

6 - Monolith

Unfortunatly that was where it all went bad. We headed inside and were immedietly jumped by a pair of Monolith (LITZ). Walter Shenoski was killed for chatting shit and I was let go. The guy at the start had been in contact with his brothers.

I went inside and met a Duty (LITZ) guy. He had been jumped by them too, managing to kill the one he'd seen.

Return

I made a return run for Cordon (LITZ), only getting into trouble with a dog but otherwise, made it back nice and safe.

Notes

  • Monolith tried to assassinate me. Found out later I was a HVT on their list, because I helped people.
